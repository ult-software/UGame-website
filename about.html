UGame allows users to create a library of their games, PC, mobile, console, wherever, with full customizability, then the ability to launch and track their time in all of those games.


UGame started development in January 2019, starting as "The Library of Yeep". It started as a very simple website that was just a table of info. Over time the project became more and more ambitious, becoming what it is today.
